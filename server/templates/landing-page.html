Frequently Asked Questions

What is Probaly? Probaly is an AI sports analysis app that turns historical data and live match statistics into accurate, probability-scored predictions across the NFL, NBA, MLB, NHL, Premier League, MMA, tennis and golf.

Are the sports predictions free? Yes. Probaly publishes one free AI prediction every day. Probaly Premium unlocks the full slate of daily AI predictions and advanced probability insights.

How accurate are Probaly's AI predictions? Each prediction includes a probability score, a confidence rating and the factors that drove the model's decision so you can judge accuracy yourself. Probaly tracks every prediction in a public history so users can verify long-term performance.

What data does Probaly analyse? Probaly's AI model processes recent form, head-to-head records, player statistics, injury reports and live match data to generate probability-scored predictions for every game.

Which sports does Probaly cover? Probaly covers eight major sports: football (NFL and college), basketball (NBA and EuroLeague), baseball (MLB), ice hockey (NHL), soccer (Premier League and major leagues), MMA (UFC), tennis (ATP and WTA) and golf (PGA Tour).
